    About Us

    At OZZON, we specialize in creating exceptional homes and attractive commercial buildings using shipping containers. With our innovative approach to construction, we transform these steel boxes into contemporary, sustainable living and commercial spaces that are both stylish and eco-friendly. Join us as we embark on a journey to redefine traditional building and embrace the future of sustainable housing.

    Our Mission

    At OZZON, our mission is to revolutionize the construction industry by providing sustainable, affordable, and innovative homes and offices constructed from shipping containers. We aim to create living spaces that not only meet the unique needs and aspirations of our clients but also contribute to a greener and more sustainable future.

    Our Vision

    Our vision is to be the leading provider of container homes and commercial buildings, setting new standards in design, functionality, and sustainability. We envision a world where container buildings are recognized as the epitome of modern living, offering architectural excellence, energy efficiency, and minimal environmental impact.

    Luxurious BedRoom Container

    Technical Specification

    • Size and Layout: Shipping containers come in various sizes, such as 20-foot or 40-foot lengths. The size you choose will depend on your requirements and available space. The layout can be customized to include a bedroom area, a small sitting or lounge area, and possibly a bathroom or kitchenette.
    • Insulation and Climate Control: Adequate insulation is essential to ensure comfortable living conditions within the container. High-quality insulation materials are used maintain a stable indoor temperature and reduce energy consumption for heating or cooling. Climate control systems, such as air conditioning or heating units, can be installed to regulate the temperature as needed.
    • Storage Solutions: Since space can be limited within a container, incorporating smart storage solutions is important. Built-in closets, under-bed storage, and multifunctional furniture pieces can help maximize the available space while maintaining a clean and organized look.

    Modern Kitchen Container

    Technical Specification

    • Compliance with regulations:   Kitchen containers can be designed to meet health and safety regulations specific to the food service industry.
    • Durability: Shipping containers are built to withstand harsh conditions during transportation, making them inherently durable.
    • Branding and marketing opportunities: It can be customized with branding elements such as logos, colors, and signage, helping to promote and reinforce the brand identity of food businesses.
    • Scalability: It can be easily expanded or downsized based on changing needs. Additional containers can be added to accommodate increased food production or dine-in area, while excess containers can be removed if requirements decrease.

    Executive Office Container

    Technical Specification

    • Mobility and flexibility: Office containers are designed to be easily transported and relocated. They can be transported to different sites or moved within a site, offering flexibility for businesses or construction projects that require temporary or mobile office spaces.
    • Quick setup Compared to traditional office construction, office containers can be set up quickly. They are pre-fabricated structures that only need to be transported to the desired location and placed on a foundation.
    • Aesthetics and branding: Office containers can be customized with company logos, colors, and designs, allowing businesses to maintain their branding even in temporary or remote locations.
    • Environmentally friendly: Reusing shipping containers for office spaces is an eco-friendly alternative to constructing new buildings. It reduces the demand for new construction materials and minimizes waste.

    Elegant Dining Container
